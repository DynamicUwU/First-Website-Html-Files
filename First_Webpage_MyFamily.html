MY FAMILY

My family is ordinary, like your typical Filipino family. I have my parents and a little brother that’s 3 years younger than me.
We usually get along well and if there are any quarrels it often gets fixed pretty quickly.
I don’t know what else to say really, I love them, and they feel the same towards me.

There is also my relatives, I do get along with most of them well but mostly on my mother’s side as I barely get to socialize with anyone on my father’s side.
Again, standard family stuff.

And in case anyone’s wondering, I don’t have any grudges nor problems with any of my relatives or my family that I live with as of writing this.
And I hope I don’t get into any in the future.
